Rise of Cardi B

By Travis Wolf

<p>The first time I heard Cardi B’s voice was in 2014.</p>

<p>“It’s cold outside, but I’m still looking like a thotty, because a hoe never gets cold.”</p><p>She was strutting down a hotel hallway, preaching this phrase in a Vine, clipped from a <a href="https://www.instagram.com/p/vz6zX5zHbv/?hl=en">video she uploaded to her Instagram</a> (<a href="https://www.instagram.com/iamcardib/?hl=en">@iamcardib</a>). The then stripper, had only posted it as promotion for a club appearance. Although the video was both popular and extremely quotable, I didn’t expect to hear Cardi B’s voice again.</p><p>Fortunately, my expectations were misguided.</p><p>By fall of this year, Cardi B’s voice was everywhere. Hardly anyone could escape the forceful beat and inspirational, catchy lyrics of her single “Bodak Yellow (Money Moves).” It’s difficult to imagine a party this year in which “Bodak Yellow” wasn’t playing.</p>
